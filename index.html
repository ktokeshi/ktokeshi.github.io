About me

I am a PhD student who belongs to Research Center for the Early Universe (RESCEU), the University of Tokyo. I am working on theoretical Cosmology of the early Universe, in particular primordial black holes (PBHs).
Keywords: Very Early Universe, Cosmic Inflation, Cosmological Perturbations, Primordial Black Holes.
